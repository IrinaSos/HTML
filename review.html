Irina

I have been using it for 2 months, I switched from 8 plus, so the phone did not cause the effect of a shovel. In general, the impressions are positive, but if you are a picky owner of 8 plus, then you will not notice any difference, except for the design. Face id does not always work, it has already been reconfigured several times, and this phenomenon has nothing to do with the presence of glasses, hats, etc. I don’t like that after recognizing me with a phone, it doesn’t unlock completely, but you still need to pull the curtain, it’s inconvenient. With touch id, this was not the case. Many applications are not yet designed for this screen, so be prepared for the fact that you will see some software, like on old plus iPhones. The camera app itself is also truncated along the frames. Stereo sound, excellent, I would say better than 8 plus, but not by much. Photos, videos are good, even in low light conditions. IOS, as always, is familiar and relaxed, everything flies, there are no complaints about this.
